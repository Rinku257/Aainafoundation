About Us

Aaina Foundation is a Non-Governmental Organization working
in the field of providing quality education in academics as well as
skill development classes to the underprivileged kids and youth
of India. Education is an indispensable tool to fight poverty and
hence every child deserves to get basic education in his life.Aaina
organizes a comprehensive education program where slum children
will be provided free of cost education by the professsional brand
also learn skills of their interest by great artists that will help
them to achieve mastery in the respective field.
